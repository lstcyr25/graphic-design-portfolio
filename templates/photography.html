My Photography

Photography became a large interest of mine alongside graphic design. In the beginning I didn’t see much in it, but over time I’ve grown to see how diverse the photography industry is and the importance of it in different workplaces. I’ve used a variety of techniques to create multiple series that explore topics such as still life, portraiture, shutter speed, etc. I used Photoshop to edit the images by adjusting the colors, details, removing items, and to overall finalize them.
